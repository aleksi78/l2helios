General Information:
Min/Max Players: 1/1
Type: PvE
Instance time: 60 Minutes
Reuse: 24 Hours
Difficulty: Easy
Status: Available
Description:
The main goal of this instance is to kill as many mobs as possible in 30 minutes. During that time you must collect points of the mobs and must try not to loose them. At the end you can trade your points for Tissue Energy Crystals which can be changed on the Dimensional Door.


Reward Information: On this instance you will get Shiny Coins, the amount depends on how many mobs you kill.
These coins can be used at the Dimensional Door Shop.

Available Actions: Enter to the instance.
