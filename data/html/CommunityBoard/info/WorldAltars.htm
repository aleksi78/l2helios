World Altars

These altars can be used to spawn the ancient creatures, these creatures are so strong and only the true heroes will be able to beat them.
Each time one of these ancient creatures is killed, the altar where was trapped disappears and appears again after few minutes at other location around the world.
All these ancient creatures drops Blue Eva that can be used on the Altars shop located at each altar.
Only a party of 4 non dual boxed players will be able to summon an ancient creature.
