Baylor's Cavern:

General Information:
Min/Max Players: 7/7
Type: PvE
Instance time: 60 Minutes
Reuse: Every day at 6:30AM
Difficulty: Hard
Status: Available
Description:
Baylor is stronger than ever and the raid battle has changed. The revamped Baylor takes place in Crystal Caverns raid and occurs in three stages. In the first stage, characters fight two Baylors. Sometimes an alarm will spawn, which must be destroyed quickly before it makes Baylor stronger. The amount of time characters spend in stage one affects how Baylor behaves in stage two. In stage three, characters can receive help from a Crystalline Golem if they can free him from prison. Baylor is strongest in this stage. Characters may become imprisoned as well, and will need help from the group in order to be freed.


Reward Information: On this instance you will get Shiny Coin, the amount depends on the instance difficulty and instance minimum players.
These coins can be used at the Dimensional Door Shop.

Available Actions: Enter to the instance.
