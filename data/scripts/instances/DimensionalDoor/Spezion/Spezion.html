Spezion's Prison:

General Information:
Min/Max Players: 7/7
Type: PvE
Instance time: 60 Minutes
Reuse: Every day at 6:30AM
Difficulty: Hard
Status: Available
Description:
Spezion will be immune to damage till some player remove his shield with the cannons. In order to remove his celestial shield Spezion should be on a specific place, and some player should use the cannon to shoot him with a magic skill.


Reward Information: On this instance you will get Shiny Coin, the amount depends on the instance difficulty and instance minimum players.
These coins can be used at the Dimensional Door Shop.

Available Actions: Enter to the instance (Normal). Enter to the instance (Extreme).
